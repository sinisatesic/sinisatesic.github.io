Testing, Research and Evaluation

We develop language assessment and measure foreign language proficiency tests based on the ILR scale (from Basic to Advance Level) and ACTFL Proficiency Guidelines (from Novice to Superior). We have test raters in Reading, listening, writing, and speaking skills are offered in different languages.

Learn More

Research-based teaching methodologies based on the needs of your organization:


  • High-level proficiency teaching training for high ILR levels
  • Language assessment and culture intelligent assessment tools based on the ILR scale (from basic to advance level)
  • OPI test-taking preparation
  • Diagnostic Assessment Tools for testing
  • ACTFL proficiency guidelines, (from novice to superior) for measuring student performance

  • Evaluation of the Human Environment and analyses of student demonstration of extensive depth and breadth of expertise in advanced concepts.
  • Culture Intelligence Assessment
  • Assessment of regional analysis and cultural analysis.
