Professional Development Workshop

CCILS offers language training workshops and professional development opportunities for all critical languages, ESL workshop, consecutive and simultaneous translation training, Offered on site or virtually. We are dedicated to serve the assortment of business, government, and community.

Learn More

At CCILS, our teaching experts work with individuals or groups in areas of language and cultural training and development.  Our workshops are adapted for our customer’s needs and can be delivered in seminar format at your site or ours, in a virtual synchronous classroom using a common meeting platform, or as custom-designed online training modules.

ESL Teaching Proficiency Workshop:

This workshop helps ESL instructors to teach and design curriculum for students requiring a higher-level English proficiency.

Teachers Professional Development Workshop:

Our highly qualified advisors and expertise provide training and help teachers to create a cross- cultural classroom environment.
